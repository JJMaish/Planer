Procession of the Holy Blood

4.7

Religious Event

This UNESCO-recognized procession has been held annually since 1304. Hundreds of participants in medieval costumes reenact biblical scenes and carry the relic of the Holy Blood through the streets of Bruges, followed by a solemn religious ceremony.
