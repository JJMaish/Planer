Important Information

  • Tours operate daily from 10:00 to 18:00

  • Tickets can be purchased on-site
  • Cash and cards accepted
  • No advance booking required

Weather Policy

  • Tours may be cancelled during severe weather
  • Boat tours operate year-round except during ice
  • Horse carriages may not operate in extreme heat
  • Bike tours may be cancelled during heavy rain
