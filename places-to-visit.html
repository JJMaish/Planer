Must-Visit Places in Bruges

Discover the medieval charm and hidden gems of Bruges

Belfry Tower

Market Square

Medieval bell tower offering panoramic city views. Climb 366 steps to reach the top of this UNESCO World Heritage site.

Open: 9:00 - 17:00 Historic Landmark

Market Square

City Center

Historic central plaza surrounded by colorful guild houses and the Provincial Court. The heart of medieval Bruges.

Always Open Historic Plaza

Church of Our Lady

Mariastraat

Gothic church housing Michelangelo's Madonna and Child sculpture and impressive art collection.

Open: 9:30 - 17:00 Religious Site

Minnewater Lake

South Bruges

Romantic lake known as the 'Lake of Love', surrounded by beautiful parks and historic buildings.

Always Open Park & Lake

Basilica of the Holy Blood

Burg Square

Famous for housing a relic of the Holy Blood of Jesus Christ. Features Romanesque and Gothic architecture.

Open: 10:00 - 16:30 Religious Site

Groeningemuseum

Dijver 12

Museum showcasing six centuries of Belgian visual art, including Flemish Primitive masterpieces.

Open: 9:30 - 17:00 Art Museum
